Araku Valley

Araku is a hill station with green valleys and waterfalls. It is peaceful and cool, and the train journey to Araku is beautiful.

Taj Mahal (Agra)

The Taj Mahal is a beautiful white marble building. It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife. It is one of the most famous monuments in the world.

Varanasi

Varanasi is a holy city on the banks of the Ganga River. People come here to pray and take a holy dip. The evening Ganga Aarti is very famous.

Jaipur is called the Pink City. It has many forts and palaces. Tourists love places like Hawa Mahal and Amer Fort.
